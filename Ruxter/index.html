What is Ruxter?

Ruxter gives you the power to reach your mobile customers. With easy-to-use DIY templates and tools, Ruxter is a one-stop-shop for everything you need to establish your mobile online presence.

Take the cost and technical hassles out of mobile marketing - with Ruxter, it’s easy to make mobile part of your business, organization, or event with all the features you need:

  • Build your personal mobile site in minutes.
  • Be your own Groupon® with daily deals.
  • Create and distribute mobile-optimized coupons via text message and social networks.
  • Instant online mobile loyalty cards. (coming soon!)
  • Social media integration to help you and your customers spread the word!
  • Fast & effective text marketing tools.
  • Charts & metrics to count your fans and followers.
  • Integrated QR code with every mobile site!

Ruxter mobile sites work with any mobile phone. Build it once and you have a web app that performs great on iPhone®, Android®, Windows® and Blackberry® smartphones. (Older flip/feature phones too!)

Mobile Coupons

Ruxter is known for their mobile coupons. There are five easy steps in creating your own custom coupon:

  • Step 1: Build your custom coupon with your logo and photos to show the amazing deals you have available.

  • Step 2: Create up to three coupons at the same time, each with its own start and expiration date. Customize each with an image, or use our stock images.

  • Step 3: Distribute your coupon via Facebook® and Twitter®, your email contacts, and to mobile subscribers of your Ruxter mobile marketing campaign.

  • Step 4: Once your coupon is published, your customers can claim it directly on their mobile phone, no paper to print or clip. They just click ‘Claim’, and create or login to their Ruxter account.

  • Step 5: Ruxter makes mobile coupon redemption a snap by letting you use any camera-equipped smartphone or tablet to validate the coupon.
